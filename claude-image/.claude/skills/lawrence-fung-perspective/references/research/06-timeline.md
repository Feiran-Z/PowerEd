## Active Programs (as of April 2026)

- **Stanford Neurodiversity Project (SNP)** — founded 2018
- **Neurodiversity Clinic** — founded 2018
- **Neurodiversity at Work Program (NaW)** — founded 2019; ~800 candidates served
- **Neurodiverse Student Support Program (NSSP)** — founded 2019
- **SNP-REACH** high school camp — founded 2020; 6th year in 2026
- **Stanford Neurodiversity Summit** — launched 2020; 7th planned Sep 2026
- **Fung Lab** — ongoing neuroimaging and community-based research
- **SNP-BRIDGE** college program — 2026 pilot
