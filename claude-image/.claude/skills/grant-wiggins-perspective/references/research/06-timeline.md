# Grant Wiggins: Life Timeline

## Overview
Grant Wiggins (November 10, 1950 – May 26, 2015) was an American educational consultant, author, and co-creator of the Understanding by Design (UbD) framework.

---

## Timeline

| Year(s) | Event | Significance |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| 1950 | Born in New York | |
| 1968-1972 | BA from St. John's College, Annapolis (Great Books program) | Shaped his conviction that deep engagement with foundational ideas matters more than coverage. Learned physics through Newton's Principia, geometry through Euclid. |
| ~1972-1986 | High school English and philosophy teacher (14 years) | Practitioner credibility. He always said "I've been in the classroom." |
| 1981-1987 | Ed.M. and Ed.D. from Harvard Graduate School of Education | Combined practitioner experience with academic rigor. Dual identity defined his career. |
| Late 1980s | Left classroom to work with Ted Sizer's Coalition of Essential Schools (CES) at Brown University | Transition from teaching one classroom to influencing many. Became Director of Research on Curriculum and Teaching. |
| 1989 | Published "A True Test: Toward More Authentic and Equitable Assessment" (Phi Delta Kappan) and "Teaching to the (Authentic) Test" (Educational Leadership) | Landmark articles that launched the authentic assessment movement. Established him as a national voice. |
| Late 1980s/Early 1990s | Founded CLASS (Center on Learning, Assessment, and School Structure) | Created institutional home for his reform ideas. Consulted with schools, districts, and state education departments. |
| 1993 | Published *Assessing Student Performance* | First major book systematizing his assessment framework. |
| 1998 | Co-published *Understanding by Design* (1st edition) with Jay McTighe (ASCD) | Launched the most influential curriculum design framework in education. |
| 1998 | Published *Educative Assessment* | Extended his assessment framework. |
| 1999 | Published *The Understanding by Design Handbook* with Jay McTighe | Practical companion to UbD. |
| 2002 | Founded Authentic Education (AE) with wife Denise Wilbur | Transitioned from nonprofit CLASS to consulting firm. Provided sustained partnerships with schools worldwide. |
| 2005 | Expanded 2nd Edition of *Understanding by Design* | Updated framework with more tools, templates, and refined concepts. |
| 2007 | Published *Schooling by Design* with Jay McTighe | Extended UbD from individual units to whole-school reform. |
| 2011 | Published *The Understanding by Design Guide to Creating High-Quality Units* | Practical module-based guide with UbD Template Version 2.0. Introduced A-M-T construct. |
| 2012 | Published *The Understanding by Design Guide to Advanced Concepts* | Companion guide for unit refinement and peer review. |
| 2013 | Published *Essential Questions: Opening Doors to Student Understanding* with Jay McTighe | Definitive practical guide on designing and using essential questions. |
| 2015 (May 26) | Died suddenly at age 64 from a heart condition | Day before his death, published Part 2 of his response to Daniel Willingham on reading comprehension. |
