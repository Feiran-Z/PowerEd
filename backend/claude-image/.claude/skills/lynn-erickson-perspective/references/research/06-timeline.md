# Timeline: Dr. H. Lynn Erickson

| Year | Event | Significance |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Pre-1990s | Worked as teacher, principal, curriculum director in public schools | Grounded her framework in real classroom experience |
| c. 1987 | First recognized the failure of traditional curriculum design to develop deep understanding | The "three decades ago" reference from her 2017 article |
| c. 1994 | As Curriculum Director, designed concept-based curriculum model for a school district | Practical origin of Structure of Knowledge |
| **1995** | **Published "Stirring the Head, Heart, and Soul" (1st edition)** | **Introduced Structure of Knowledge to the field** |
| c. 1996 | Lois Lanning joined her work | Beginning of long-term collaboration |
| **2001** | **"Stirring the Head, Heart, and Soul" 2nd edition** | Complete rewrite |
| **2002** | **"Concept-Based Curriculum and Instruction: Teaching Beyond the Facts"** | Developed framework further for general audience |
| **2007** | **"Concept-Based Curriculum and Instruction for the Thinking Classroom" (1st edition)** | Introduced synergistic thinking concept |
| **2008** | **"Stirring the Head, Heart, and Soul" 3rd edition** | Revamped subtitle emphasizing concept-based focus; added sections on cooperative learning, differentiation, guiding questions |
| 2010 | Contributed chapter to Robert Marzano's "On Excellence in Teaching" | Bridged CBCI with broader educational leadership |
| c. 2012 | Consulted with British Columbia Ministry of Education | BC adopts KUD framework |
| c. 2012 | Consulted with IB Organization for MYP—the Next Chapter | International reach through IB schools worldwide |
| **2013/2014** | **"Transitioning to Concept-Based Curriculum and Instruction" (with Lanning)** | **Integrated Structure of Knowledge + Structure of Process** |
| 2015 | Last personal CBCI Certification Institute | Retirement from active institute leadership |
| **2017** | **"Concept-Based Curriculum and Instruction for the Thinking Classroom" (2nd edition, with Lanning & French)** | **Definitive statement; added inquiry and teacher development chapters** |
| 2018 | Concept-Based Inquiry in Action published (Marschall & French, drawing from Erickson's framework) | The next generation of CBCI |
| Present | Retired; lives in Everett, Washington | Legacy sustained through PLI certification institute |
| 2025-2026 | Corwin/Sage still actively publishing/distributing her works | Continued demand for her framework |
| 2026/2027 | CBCI Trainer Certification Institute currently accepting applications via PLI | Her model continues to be taught worldwide |

## Verification
Dates for major book publications are confirmed through Sage/Corwin publisher records and ERIC database. Specific years for consulting engagements are from author biographies. Retirement status from PLI institute application page.
